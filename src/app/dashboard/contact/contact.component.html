Frequently Asked Questions

Find detailed support answers regarding course enrollments, sensory accessibility features, haptics, testing environments, and government scholarships.

How do I register and enroll in a course?
Registration is completely free and accessible. Click the "Login / Sign Up" button in the navigation bar to create a student account by entering your details. Once registered, log in, browse the "Courses" page, select any course matching your curriculum (e.g. BCA, B.Sc IT), and click the "Enroll Now" button. The course will instantly link to your personal student dashboard.
What accessibility controls does Siksha Setu provide?
We provide a global floating Accessibility Widget (represented by a palette/wheel icon at the bottom-right). Clicking this widget opens a drawer enabling:
  • High Contrast: Toggle Yellow-on-Black or White-on-Black modes for low-vision comfort.
  • Dyslexia Friendly Font: Change the global typeface to Lexend for enhanced readability.
  • Custom Spacing: Choose between Loose or Extra Word Spacing and double-line heights.
  • Large Cursor: Swap to a high-visibility red pointing cursor.
  • Focus Ruler: Activate a horizontal reading guide that tracks your cursor.
 How does the Braille display translation engine work?
Siksha Setu features a built-in Braille converter. When visually impaired learners view lesson resources, they can toggle "Braille View". The system translates letters, numbers, and symbols into standard 6-dot Braille cells instantly. We are currently developing integration APIs to transmit this converted Braille data directly to external refreshable physical Braille hardware displays via Bluetooth/USB.
What Sign Language resources are available?
To support deaf and hard-of-hearing learners, our core video player contains sign language sync options. Enrolled students can activate sign language overlay windows that render synchronized hand-gesture guides alongside course video modules. Full closed caption controls are also adjustable inside the reader settings.
How do I take tests and download certificates?
At the end of each course, students can launch the course evaluation test. Upon completing and passing the test with a score of 60% or more, our backend dynamically stamps a verified completion certificate. This certificate appears under the "Certificates" block of your Student Dashboard, ready for instant download or sharing.
What is the Secure Test Environment?
Our evaluation tests launch in a secure mode to ensure certificate integrity. In this state, the window detects exit triggers, locks page context copy/paste actions, detects tab switching, and provides auditory warnings if focus is lost. It ensures students complete assessments fairly and autonomously.
What Government Schemes are integrated here?
Siksha Setu hosts a verified index of national and state-level schemes for disabled students (e.g. AICTE Saksham Scholarship, Post-Matric Scholarships, ADIP Scheme for free aid distribution). On your student dashboard, click "Government Schemes" to check eligibility details, required income brackets, and directly access the official application portals.
How do I edit my profile details?
You can edit fields like address or contact numbers under "My Profile" at any time. However, to prevent academic fraud, secure fields (like full name or disability registration files) are locked once verified. To edit these, submit a change request through the profile editor form; your platform Administrator will review and approve the upload.
