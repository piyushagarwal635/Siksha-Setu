🌟 About Siksha Setu

Empowering Every Mind

Siksha Setu represents a new paradigm in digital learning. We build assistive tools and multi-sensory formats that break down physical and digital barriers, ensuring every specially-abled learner can read, listen, touch, and learn without constraints.

The Problem

Over 90% of traditional online learning systems fail basic web accessibility compliance. Visually impaired students are forced to rely on external readers, while deaf students lack integrated sign explanations, leaving them isolated in the classroom.

Our Vision

To create a world where sensory, cognitive, or physical differences do not determine a person's educational limits. We envision an educational framework that seamlessly conforms to the individual, rather than forcing the individual to adapt to the interface.

Our Mission

To build and scale Indian education's first multi-sensory classroom. By blending real-time voice recognition, sign language sync, dynamic text-to-speech, and virtual tactile Braille interfaces, we aim to make high-quality independent study accessible to all.

Our Core Pillars

Total Inclusivity

Customizing layouts, text dimensions, line spacing, and sound feedback so students with visual, auditory, and reading differences feel comfortable.

Smart Assist

Using speech recognition voice commands, automated screen readers, and interactive text highlighting rulers to aid focused study.

Gamified Joy

Spiced-up practice quizzes featuring audio feedback, instant alerts, and interactive particle confetti to make studying fun and exciting.

Assistive Tech & Future Roadmap

We leverage cutting-edge tech standards and outline a realistic path to fully autonomous, accessible learning.

Braille & 3D Pin Hardware Support

Seamlessly connect your virtual or physical 3D Braille display. Our system automatically translates rich text into Braille patterns in real-time, allowing tactile learning at your fingertips. We are currently working with hardware manufacturers to support physical refreshable Braille pins directly.

Voice & Keyword Navigation

Navigate the entire platform using natural voice commands or simple keyboard shortcuts. Simply say "Go to courses" or type key phrases to move around effortlessly without reliance on physical pointers.

Built-in Screen Reader Helper

No need for external software. Our advanced native screen reader announces contextually aware actions, reads study materials aloud, and guides you through complex test environments seamlessly.

AI-Driven Learning Roadmap

Developing integrated AI study planners that analyze active user streaks, auto-generate voice-synthesized flashcards, and adapt quizzes to focus areas based on accessibility needs.

Meet the Founder

Piyush Agarwal

Creator & Lead Developer

Siksha Setu is the passion project of Piyush Agarwal, a dedicated developer who envisioned a platform where technology breaks down barriers instead of building them. Driven by the belief that education is a universal right, Piyush built Siksha Setu from the ground up to ensure that students with disabilities—whether visual, auditory, or cognitive—have a level playing field in the digital world.
