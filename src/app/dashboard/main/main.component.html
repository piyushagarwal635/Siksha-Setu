Our Mission & Approach

Standard e-learning suites frequently fail to accommodate sensory differences. We engineered Siksha Setu to offer a barrier-free digital classroom using modern web standards.

The Challenge

A lack of standardized assistive frameworks in modern e-learning ecosystems.

The Innovation

A unified platform combining text-to-speech, voice command listeners, Lexend fonts, and real-time tactile translator components.

The Impact

Enabling students of all sensory profiles to study, take tests, and track accomplishments independently.

Why Choose Siksha Setu?

  • Native Text-To-Speech engine without requiring external screen readers.
  • Content format adaptations including Text, Audio descriptions, and Sign language.
  • Premium, distraction-free interface matching top modern SaaS standards.
